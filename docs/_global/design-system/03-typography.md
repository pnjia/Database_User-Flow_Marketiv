# Tipografi (Typography)

Aturan penggunaan font, ukuran teks, dan line height.

## Font Family
- **Heading**: `Newsreader`, serif (Untuk judul, nilai branding, elegan)
- **Body**: `Inter`, sans-serif (Untuk teks deskripsi, input, label, mudah dibaca)

## Heading (Newsreader)
| Elemen | Ukuran (Font Size) | Ketebalan (Weight) | Line Height |
|---|---|---|---|
| H1 | 32px | Bold (700) | 1.2 |
| H2 | 24px | Bold (700) | 1.3 |
| H3 | 20px | SemiBold (600)| 1.4 |

## Body Text (Inter)
| Elemen | Ukuran (Font Size) | Ketebalan (Weight) | Line Height |
|---|---|---|---|
| Body Large | 16px | Regular (400) | 1.5 |
| Body Medium | 14px | Regular (400) | 1.5 |
| Body Small | 12px | Regular (400) | 1.5 |

## Khusus (Labels & Buttons)
- **Label Medium**: 14px, Medium (500)
- **Label Small**: 12px, Medium (500)
- **Button Text**: 16px, SemiBold (600)
